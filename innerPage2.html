About Us

MA Engineering Solution is an Electro Mechanical Solution & Service provider company in Bangladesh. MA Engineering Solution is pleased to introduce its self as a Solution & Service provider of Low Voltage & Medium Voltage System (up to 36KV).

We are manufacturing & assembling HT & LT switchgear, PFI Plant, Distribution Panel, Metering Panel, Automatice Transfer Switch (ATS), Generator Synchronization Panel, Our manufactured switchgear panel & distribution board comply with IEC, BNBC Accord, Allience & Buereau Veritas safety standard.

Moreover, we have a widespread reputation for providing an excellent service to our clients through proper understanding of customer needs and translate it into quality designs. To ensure quality solution & service, MA Engineering Solution has a strong technical team for industrial, commercial as well as residential projects.

In a nutshell, MA Engineering Solution devotes itself to achieve customer satisfication by providing compact, economical & environment freandly technical solution.
